Saylani Welfare relies solely on generous donations from individuals and organisations. We pride ourselves in careful planning and allocation of funds to ensure that the right set of resources reaches the right group of people.
Saylani Welfare runs more than 30 different projects including interest-free financial aid, educational projects for children, employment and monthly allowance schemes, medical aid and utility bills payment covers.
Currently under construction are central building of Saylani Welfare in Pakistan, as well as clinic and diagnostic laboratory for those suffering from diabetes and hepatitis.
Many Pakistani citizens residing in villages and towns live below the poverty line. Deprived of basic necessities such as food, clean water and medical treatment, many families require urgent assistance.
